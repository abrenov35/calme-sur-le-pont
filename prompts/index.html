Base Prompt de style commun à toutes les illustrations ▼ 
Black and white ink engraving illustration, highly detailed cross-hatching and fine line work, 19th century book illustration style. Set inside or around a traditional wooden sailing ship. Dramatic chiaroscuro lighting from a single oil lantern. Extreme attention to texture : visible wood grain on planks and crates, twisted rope details, worn fabric. Deep shadows with rich blacks, bright highlights on key elements. No color whatsoever, pure black ink on white paper. Horizontal format, slightly wider than tall.

Chapitre 1 Main sur une caisse dans la cale ▼ 
[PROMPT BASE] + A weathered captain's hand resting firmly on a wooden crate inside a ship's dark hold. Ropes, barrels and other crates visible in the background. A single lantern hangs from a beam, casting dramatic light. The gesture is calm, deliberate, decisive.
Chapitre 2 Pont vide, mer calme, capitaine immobile ▼ 
[PROMPT BASE] + The empty deck of a sailing ship on a perfectly calm sea. Sails perfectly set, ropes motionless. The captain stands alone near the helm, hands relaxed at his sides, looking at the still water. Golden midday light reflects on the glassy sea surface.
Chapitre 3 Marins qui discutent, voile qui pend ▼ 
[PROMPT BASE] + A group of sailors gathered on deck, talking and gesturing animatedly among themselves. In the background, a single sail hangs slightly loose from its rigging. The captain watches from a distance, calm and observant.
Chapitre 4 Table de navigation, compas, instruments ▼ 
[PROMPT BASE] + Close-up of a ship's compass and an open nautical chart spread on a wooden table. A captain's hand hovers above the compass needle without touching it. A lantern illuminates the scene. Fog visible through a small porthole in the background.
Chapitre 5 Vent contraire, capitaine calme à la barre ▼ 
[PROMPT BASE] + Same black and white ink engraving style. Keep the captain at the helm scene, but tone down the storm significantly. The sea should be rough but not violent — choppy waves, sails straining sideways, the ship leaning slightly. No dramatic crashing waves. The mood is one of patience and resistance, not danger. The captain holds the wheel firmly but calmly.
Chapitre 6 Capitaine descend à la cabine, équipage sur le pont ▼ 
[PROMPT BASE] + A captain descends alone the steps from the deck to his cabin below, his hand resting on the wooden railing. Through a porthole, the night sea is visible. The crew continues working on deck above. Mood: trust, letting go, rest after long duty.
Épilogue Navire amarré au coucher du soleil ▼ 
[PROMPT BASE] + A sailing ship moored quietly at a stone dock at sunset. Sails folded, ropes at rest, the deck empty and silent. The captain stands on the dock looking back at his ship one last time. Long shadows, warm golden light fading on the water. A mood of quiet accomplishment.

Structure Structure de chaque chapitre V2 ▼ 
Pour chaque chapitre V2, développer en 4 parties :
1. LA SCÈNE — Le texte original V1 enrichi et développé. Narration maritime concrète.
2. L'INTROSPECTION — Le Capitaine se retire dans sa cabine. Sa posture intérieure face à la situation.
3. L'ÉCHO — Une interaction avec un membre de l'équipage. Ancrage humain, problématique universelle (angoisse, délégation, bruit de fond…).
4. LA NOTE DE BORD — Une maxime courte et actionnable. Ce que le lecteur emporte.

Ton : poétique pour l'immersion, clinique pour le développement personnel. Style sobre, phrases courtes, pas de jargon.
